Our Privacy Policy

Welcome to Selkirk Maintenance. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website and services. By using our platform, you agree to the practices described herein.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide when booking a service or registering on our platform, such as your name, email, phone number, and address. We may also automatically collect data through cookies and analytics tools to improve user experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to process bookings, communicate service updates, and enhance our service delivery. We may also use your data for marketing purposes, ensuring that any communication is relevant and valuable to you.

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information with trusted third-party service providers who help us operate our website and deliver our services, subject to confidentiality agreements.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or request deletion of your personal information. For any privacy concerns, please contact us at info@selkirmaintenance.com.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on our website, and your continued use of our services constitutes acceptance of those changes.
