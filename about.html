About Selkirk Maintenance

Our Story & Mission

Selkirk Maintenance was founded to address the critical need for reliable maintenance services in Nelson and Castlegar. Our innovative gig marketplace connects highly rated freelance contractors with customers who require urgent and routine maintenance work – even during after-hours, weekends, and holidays. We believe that quality service should be accessible and stress-free, which is why we focus on building trust and fostering strong community relationships.

Our founder, Marc, is renowned for his diligence and adaptability. After graduating from Selkirk College’s School of Business, he leveraged his extensive organizational and coordination skills to launch this platform. By focusing on the unique needs of the local community, Selkirk Maintenance not only aims to deliver exceptional maintenance solutions but also contributes significantly to the overall growth and prosperity of the region.

We pride ourselves on our commitment to quality, transparency, and local expertise, ensuring that every service call results in reliable, timely, and professional maintenance solutions.
